# Meeting Debrief Workflow

Execute these steps IN ORDER. Complete each step fully and show the required OUTPUT before moving to the next step.

---

## Step 1: Capture the Meeting

Ask:

> **Quick debrief:**
> - Which meeting was this?
> - What happened? (Key points)
> - What was decided?
> - Any action items or follow-ups?

**STOP and wait for answer before proceeding.**

---

- `second-brain/team.md` (if 1:1)
