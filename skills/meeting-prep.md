# Meeting Prep Workflow

Execute these steps IN ORDER. Complete each step fully and show the required OUTPUT before moving to the next step.

---

## Step 1: Identify the Meeting

Ask:

> **Which meeting are we prepping for?**
> - What's the meeting? (Name/type)
> - Who's attending?
> - When is it?

**STOP and wait for answer before proceeding.**

---

## Step 2: Load Relevant Context

Based on the meeting type, read relevant files:

**For 1:1s:** Read `second-brain/team.md` for that person's role, responsibilities, and current focus.

**For Leadership/Team meetings:** Read `second-brain/company.md` (goals, metrics) and `second-brain/projects.md`.

**For Client meetings:** Ask for client context or check inbox.

**For Advisory/Coaching:** Read `second-brain/profile.md` (objectives) and `second-brain/projects.md`.

**OUTPUT required before proceeding:**
```

**Attendee(s):** [Name(s) and role(s)]
**Their focus:** [From team.md or context provided]
**Relevant projects:** [Any active projects involving them]
**Last meeting notes:** [If available in logs/]
```

---

## Step 3: Clarify Objectives

Ask:

> **What do you want to accomplish in this meeting?**
> - Any specific decisions to make?
> - Topics you need to cover?
> - Outcomes you're hoping for?

**STOP and wait for answer before proceeding.**

---
